Experience

Club was founded

A group of SJSU students that met at the U.S Cyber Challenge (USCC) boot camp decided to come together to form an Information Security club with the help of Virginia Lehmkuhl-Dakhwe, the school's STEM Director, who at the time the event planner for USCC.

2013

Expanding Relationships

The club was able to connect with many companies such as Facebook and Cisco to help express the need for more InfoSec professionals. This marks the beginning of the club's relationship with the frontier we call the Silicon Valley

February 2015 - April 2015

The Club enters it's 4th year

With events such as DefCon, CTF's, and the passing of new officers from its founding fathers, this year marks the year the club's foundation can be said to be set in stone

May 2015 - Current
